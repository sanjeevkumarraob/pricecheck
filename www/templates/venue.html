The Grand Tarabya

Located on the seafront of Tarabya Bay, this luxurious hotel offers extensive views of Bosphorus. The Grand Tarabya Hotel is a member of the "leading hotels of the world" and has an outdoor pool, fitness centre, spa centre, and modern rooms with a balcony and free Wi-Fi. Tastefully decorated in contemporary style, the rooms of The Grand Tarabya Hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV, air conditioning and minibar. They all offer park, marina or Bosphorus views. All rooms have a modern bathroom with a bath tub, shower and free toiletries. Offering spectacular views of the Bosphorus, Tarabya Bay, and the Marina, all-day dining restaurant, The Brasserie Restaurant, serves open buffet breakfasts and a-la-carte lunch and dinner menus in international cuisines. Limani Balık is ideal to enjoy the flavours of Bosphorus seafood cuisine and have a glass of wine. Located in the main lobby, The T Lounge is famous for its traditional afternoon high teas, delicious pastries. R.E.A.D Café & Bakery specializes in homemade selections of chocolate pralines, variety of delicious pastries, sweet savouries and cupcakes. Diba Bar and Pool Bar are ideal spots to unwind with a refreshing drink. The 4000 m² Therapia Spa includes a sauna, Turkish bath and massages. There is also an indoor pool at Grand Tarabya Hotel. Ataturk International Airport is 35 km from the hotel. Free private parking is also available on site.
